Creating Date Table

To do Time Intelligence Functions, creating a date table is advisable; see below:

-Select the modelling tab

-New Table

-Write DAX Expression to create the table and press "Enter."

-Go to the Field list and right-click on the Date table, and mark as Date Table.

-A dialogue box appears, select Date and press ok

 



 - On the Model View Page

- Make a relationship between the Date and Accidental _ Drug _ Related _ Death
